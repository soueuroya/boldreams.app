About The Studio

Led by Experience

Boldreams is an independent software and game development studio led by Daniel Boldrin.

With over 14 years of production experience as a Senior Unity and Software Developer, Daniel has built and shipped games, mobile applications, VR systems, scalable multiplayer platforms, and complex enterprise software.

Our mission is to bring technical excellence and robust architecture to every project, ensuring that our products don't just look great—they perform flawlessly at scale.
